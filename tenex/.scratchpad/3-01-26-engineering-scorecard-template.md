## Dimension Reference

### Delivery

| Level | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| **Struggling (1-2)** | Misses deadlines without warning. Incomplete/untested work. Estimates off by 2x+. |
| **Meeting (3)** | Hits timelines. Flags risks early. Estimates within 25%. Work is complete on handoff. |
| **Excelling (4-5)** | Builds in buffer. Finds ways to ship faster. Unblocks others. |

### Communication

| Level | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| **Struggling (1-2)** | Surprises with known problems. Goes dark. Unclear updates. Avoids hard conversations. |
| **Meeting (3)** | Surfaces blockers within 24h. Clear status updates. Asks before assuming. |
| **Excelling (4-5)** | Identifies risks before they're problems. Clients praise their communication. Creates clarity for others. |

### Judgment

| Level | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| **Struggling (1-2)** | Over-engineers or builds wrong thing. Needs detailed instructions. Can't prioritize. |
| **Meeting (3)** | Breaks work into chunks. Researches before escalating. Proposes solutions, not problems. Scopes to actual need. |
| **Excelling (4-5)** | Finds simplest full solution. Self-directs. Pushes back on unnecessary scope. Decides well under uncertainty. |

### Craft

| Level | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| **Struggling (1-2)** | Skips docs/tests/review. Creates unaware tech debt. Ignores established patterns. |
| **Meeting (3)** | Follows standards. Writes tests. Documents work. Clean code reviews. |
| **Excelling (4-5)** | Raises the quality bar. Catches issues in review. Improves processes proactively. |

### Capacity

| Level | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| **Struggling (1-2)** | Overwhelmed, quality suffering. Over-commits and under-delivers. Burnout signs. |
| **Meeting (3)** | Sustainable pace. Can articulate workload. Pushes back when overloaded. Takes PTO. |
| **Excelling (4-5)** | Models sustainability. Helps others manage load. Absorbs surges without long-term impact. |
