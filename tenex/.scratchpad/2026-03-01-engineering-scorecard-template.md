## Dimensions

**Delivery** — How confident am I this person will hit their deadlines?
- *Struggling:* Misses deadlines without warning. Incomplete/untested work. Estimates off by 2x+.
- *Meeting:* Hits timelines. Flags risks early. Estimates within 25%. Work is complete on handoff.
- *Excelling:* Builds in buffer. Finds ways to ship faster. Unblocks others.

**Craft** — Do they build the right thing, and build it well?
- *Struggling:* Over-engineers or builds the wrong thing. Skips docs/tests/review. Can't distinguish what matters.
- *Meeting:* Scopes to actual need. Follows standards. Writes tests. Knows when "good enough" is right.
- *Excelling:* Finds the simplest full solution. Raises the quality bar. Makes good trade-offs under uncertainty.

**Ownership** — Does this person do what needs doing, even when it's not glamorous or visible?
- *Struggling:* Cherry-picks work. Leaves messes for others. Needs to be told what to do next.
- *Meeting:* Takes on tedious work without complaint. Follows through on commitments. Doesn't leave loose ends.
- *Excelling:* Volunteers for the work nobody wants. Notices what needs doing and does it. Others feel supported by them.

**Communication** — How effective is this person at communicating with clients, strategists, and the team?
- *Struggling:* Goes dark during complex work. Surprises with known problems. Poor signal-to-noise ratio. Doesn't adapt to audience.
- *Meeting:* Surfaces blockers early. Clear updates without being asked. Knows when to loop in leadership. Asks before assuming.
- *Excelling:* Clients and strategists praise working with them. Translates technical concepts. Creates clarity for the whole project.

**Capacity** — Is their current pace sustainable for the next 6 months?
- *Struggling:* Overwhelmed, quality suffering. Over-commits and under-delivers. Burnout signs.
- *Meeting:* Sustainable pace. Can articulate workload. Pushes back when overloaded. Takes PTO.
- *Excelling:* Models sustainability. Helps others manage load. Absorbs surges without long-term impact.

## Rating Scale

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Significant concern — needs immediate intervention |
| 2 | Below expectations — needs focused development |
| 3 | Meeting expectations — solid, reliable |
| 4 | Exceeding expectations — a strength |
| 5 | Exceptional — a model for others |

A "3" is good. Most people should be 3s on most dimensions.
