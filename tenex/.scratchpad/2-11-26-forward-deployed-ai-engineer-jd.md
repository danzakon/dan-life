## Raw Bullet Points

What this person actually does day-to-day / what the role looks like:

- Work directly with clients, learn how their business actually works, find where AI can make the biggest impact
- Scope and architect solutions end-to-end. Not just features, but full platforms that fundamentally improve how a business operates
- Build AI-powered systems that actually work in production. Reliable, auditable, with clean UX that keeps humans in the loop. Agents, internal tools, data pipelines, customer-facing products
- Ship across multiple clients simultaneously. Lots of context switching, parallelizing work, managing your own priorities
- Design systems, not just write code. You're an architect and an agent orchestrator, building things that are structured to scale and run real businesses, not demos
- Stay on the absolute cutting edge of AI. New models, new tooling, new techniques. Bring that knowledge to every engagement
- Learn independently and fast; the tech changes weekly and our clients pay us to know what's next
- Constantly optimize how you and the team work. Find higher-leverage tools, workflows, and patterns to move faster without sacrificing quality
- Collaborate closely with AI Strategists who own the client relationship and business context; you own the technical vision and execution
- Debate ideas, share what you're learning, push each other. We learn relentlessly and move fast
- Your output is directly measured (story points) and your comp is uncapped. The more you ship, the more you earn
