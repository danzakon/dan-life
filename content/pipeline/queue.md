# Content Queue

Posts that are written, reviewed, and approved -- waiting to be scheduled via PostBridge.

---

## Format

Each entry follows this structure:

```
### [ ] {Title or hook}

**Target date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Platform:** Twitter | LinkedIn | Both
**Priority:** high | normal | low
**Source:** {brief ID or source description}

**Twitter:**
{Twitter-length content}

**LinkedIn:**
{LinkedIn-length content with more context}

**Status:** queued | scheduled | posted
```

Status flow: `queued` -> `scheduled` (picked up by Cowork daily task) -> `posted` (confirmed by PostBridge)
