## Core Insight

In-person work's real advantage isn't collaboration or culture — it's clock speed. Co-located teams run a faster event loop: decisions, unblocks, and feedback cycles that take hours async happen in minutes face-to-face.

## Your Take

This should be multiple posts scheduled throughout the month, each exploring a different angle of the "clock speed" metaphor. Not one big piece — a drip of related takes.

## Lead Angle

Counterintuitive — reframe the remote-vs-office debate away from culture/collaboration and into execution speed. Most people arguing about this are arguing about the wrong thing.
