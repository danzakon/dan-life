## Core Insight

Software factories are the next frontier. The agent pattern will evolve into the factory pattern — measurable inputs, measurable outputs, quantifiable production. "Orchestration" is too bland. Factories produce things you can count.

## Your Take

This is a big series arc. The core thread: agents → factories → infinite configurations → swarm wars. The future isn't about wielding individual agents, it's about configuring and directing factories of agents that produce measurable output. "Orchestration" undersells it — factories are a better metaphor because they imply production, quality control, throughput, and optimization.
