## Core Insight

Agentic coding is the esports of our time. More people will code, not fewer — but as long as a skill differential matters, top players will matter. The best agentic coders will be billionaires.

## Your Take

Multiple posts and articles. The esports analogy is strong — everyone plays video games, but the top players make millions. Same thing is happening with coding. The "AI will replace developers" narrative is wrong. The right frame is: everyone becomes a coder, the best coders become exponentially more valuable.

## Lead Angle

Counterintuitive — the "AI replaces coders" crowd has the direction exactly backwards. AI makes coding universal, which makes elite coding skill MORE valuable, not less.
