## Core Insight
AI got dramatically better, but the average quality of AI-assisted work got worse. The better AI gets, the more tempting it is to do less. If you paste AI-generated output into an email, codebase, or report and it's wrong, that's on you. You clicked send. The human in the loop got lazy, and that's a choice, not a technology problem.
