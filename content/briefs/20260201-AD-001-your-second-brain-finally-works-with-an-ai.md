## Core Insight
The "second brain" concept has failed for a decade — not because the tools were bad, but because humans are terrible at consistently doing tedious organizational work. AI agents change this equation entirely: they can actually run the system you always wanted but couldn't maintain. The second brain finally works when an AI is operating it.
