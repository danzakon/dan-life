## Core Insight
Jack Dorsey's insight: to have great attention to detail, you have to limit the number of details. Vibe coding has made this dangerous — the friction that forced careful thinking is gone. Engineers add features because they can, not because they should. The defining tension of building software now is restraint. The best products of this era will be built by people who take twenty features down to five and make those five feel inevitable.
