## Core Insight
The skills that made people valuable in 2020 are not the same skills that make people valuable in 2026. Execution and knowledge got compressed by AI. Judgment, communication, and taste expanded. Most people haven't updated their mental model of what "being good at your job" means. The skills becoming less important are the ones schools focus on most; the ones becoming more important are the hardest to teach.

## Reference Material

Skills becoming MORE important: expressive writing, rapid document triage, taste and judgment, knowing what to ask, verification and fact-checking, system thinking across AI interactions, cross-domain fluency, emotional regulation under cognitive automation.

Skills becoming LESS important: rote knowledge retention, first-draft production, manual research and compilation, format and polish, translation between formats.
