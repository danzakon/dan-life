### 2-28-26 | AI Engineering

Jack Dorsey says that in order to have great attention to detail, the thing that matters is limiting the amount of details and then making sure every detail is perfect. This connects to the new world of vibe coding where you can create so much complexity in products so fast. The instinct most people have is to make things more and more complex and keep adding. A lot of vibe coded products have tons and tons of features, but none of them are perfect. The real skill is not going to be how much you can create, but reducing what you are creating to the smallest number of features or details to make that product perfect. This type of intelligent design or intelligent software engineering is really about refinement, quality control, quality assurance, testing, iterating, reducing entropy, and reducing slop, rather than creating more and more.
