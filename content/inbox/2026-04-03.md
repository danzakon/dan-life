### Executive Summary

Matthew Gallagher built Medvi from $20K and AI tools into a $1.8B telehealth company with 2 employees. The formula: ~70% market/timing (GLP-1 drugs), ~20% thin-layer architecture (rent all regulated infrastructure), ~10% AI tooling. Research across 5 parallel threads identified the replicable pattern and ranked 13 opportunity areas. Top picks: immigration services (closest Medvi analog), Ozempic-adjacent services, small business formation, and student loan optimization.
