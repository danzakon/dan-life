# Content

Social media and written content focused on AI engineering, technology, and business topics.

---

## Purpose

This directory houses drafts, ideas, and published content for social media and other platforms. The goal is to share insights and build a presence in the AI/tech space.

---

## Content Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| LinkedIn posts | Professional insights, industry commentary |
| Twitter/X threads | Technical takes, quick observations |
| Blog drafts | Longer-form technical writing |
| Video scripts | YouTube or short-form video outlines |

---

## Structure

```
content/
├── .scratchpad/           # Active drafts and ideas
│   └── .history/          # Archived drafts
├── published/             # Final versions of published content (optional)
└── CLAUDE.md
```

---

## Workflow

1. **Capture** — New ideas go in `.scratchpad/` with date prefix
2. **Draft** — Develop the idea with outline and key points
3. **Review** — Polish language, check for clarity
4. **Publish** — Post to platform, optionally move to `published/`
5. **Archive** — Old drafts move to `.history/` when scratchpad exceeds 15 files

---

## File Format

```markdown
# {Title}

**Platform:** LinkedIn | Twitter | Blog | Video
**Status:** [ ] Draft | [ ] Review | [ ] Published
**Published:** {date or N/A}

---

{Content here}

---

## Notes
- Background context, research links, etc.
```
