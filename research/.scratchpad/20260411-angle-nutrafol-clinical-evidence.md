# Nutrafol: Clinical Evidence Quality

**Category:** Technical  
**Date Started:** 2026-04-11  
**Status:** [x] Complete

---

## Summary

Nutrafol has peer-reviewed trials, but the strongest recent men’s RCT lost its prespecified primary endpoint (terminal hair counts via HairMetrix) to protocol deviations; published outcomes lean on blinded global photography ratings and questionnaires. Earlier work includes single-arm / single-blind designs. Most studies exclude concurrent minoxidil/finasteride, so external validity to aggressive combination therapy is weak.

## Key Findings

- [Farris et al.,2025](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11701407/): 6-month multicenter DBRPC, n=112 enrolled, 85 completers; **primary endpoint not analyzable**; secondary endpoints favored active (e.g., ~79% investigator “improved” growth at day 180 vs ~51% placebo); study funded by Nutraceutical Wellness; corresponding author @nutrafol.com.
- [Stephens et al., 2022](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8903234/): 6-month **single-blind, single-arm** prospective study; investigator and self-assessments improved; company-sponsored.
- Trials typically **exclude** minoxidil/finasteride use for months before enrollment — **no RCT evidence** for Nutrafol **on top of** pharmacologic AGA therapy.
